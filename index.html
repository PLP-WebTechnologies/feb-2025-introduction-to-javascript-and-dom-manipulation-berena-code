Things you need to do before moving out:

Moving out is a big step! Here are six key things to do before you make the move:

  • Create a Budget & Save Money
  • Find a Suitable Place to Live
  • Secure a Stable Income
  • Buy Essential Furniture & Household Items
  • Understand Legal & Rental Agreements
  • Develop Basic Life Skills

Are you planning to move out soon, or just preparing for the future?

Either way, it's important to be prepared and have a plan in place.
